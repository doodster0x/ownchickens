Infrastructure tiers

Pick the setup that matches your budget

There are three sane ways to build your first chicken setup: buy it fast, build it cheaper, or source materials locally. Tier 1 is the easy Amazon route for people who want fewer decisions and faster setup.

T1

Amazon ready-to-buy

Fastest path. Buy the core system online, compare reviews carefully, and expect to reinforce weak points.

T2

Budget quality build

Cheaper for handy people. Buy lumber, hardware cloth, roofing, fasteners, and build a better structure.

T3

Local sourcing

Lowest cash cost, highest time cost. Pallets, sheds, marketplace finds, Craigslist, and local salvage.

No-waste feeder

Wasted feed attracts rodents. Look for covered, hanging, treadle, or port-style feeders that keep feed dry and harder to scratch out.
